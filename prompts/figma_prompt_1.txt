PROMPT 1:
Design a clean, responsive Figma prototype for TaskWise, a smart team task and collaboration manager for students and small teams.
Include these key screens:
1. Dashboard/Home: Overview of projects, task stats, and quick actions.

2. Task Creation: Simple form with title, description, due date, priority, and assign-to options.

3. Task Tracking: Kanban or list view with status (To-Do, In Progress, Done) and filters.

4. Projects/Groups: Organize tasks under projects or categories with color tags.

5. Profile & Settings: Manage user info, preferences, and notifications.

6. Analytics/Reports: Show task completion rate and upcoming deadlines.

Design style:
   * Minimal, modern, and productivity-focused.

   * Colors: Blue (#2563EB), Green (#10B981), Light Gray (#F3F4F6), Dark Text (#111827).

   * Fonts: Poppins for headings, Inter for body text.

   * Use icons for tasks, alerts, and navigation.

   * Responsive layout for desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Goal:
 Deliver an intuitive prototype that enables users to create, assign, track, and visualize team tasks efficiently.
