General Prompt:
"Perform a strategic business analysis for [Product or Company Name] by following these steps:
1. Competitor Identification:
   * Find the top three to five direct and indirect competitors in the same market.
   * Provide a brief overview of each competitor’s offerings, unique selling points, and market share.
2. Comparative Analysis:
   * Create a detailed comparative matrix between [Product or Company Name] and its main competitors, focusing on:
      * Key features and service differentiation
      * Pricing structure and value proposition
      * Customer satisfaction levels, reviews, and loyalty programs
      * Technology, partner/integration ecosystem, and scalability
      * Recent innovations or expansion strategies
3. SWOT Analysis:
   * Perform a SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) for [Product or Company Name].
   * (Optional) Summarize notable SWOT highlights of the main competitor to contextualize the comparison.
4. Strategic Synthesis and Recommendations:
   * Based on your findings, identify the competitive advantages, areas of risk, and strategic opportunities for [Product or Company Name].
   * Conclude with actionable recommendations for defending or growing its market position."
